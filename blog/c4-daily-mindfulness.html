Daily Mindfulness

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

I think it is good to be a listener, but I found it hard to be. Especially these days, when I meet my friends because I have so many things that I want to tell, I was not a good listener. In this chapter, it explains that I need to do mindfulness listening for others as a gift, and I also found it helpful to understand myself that I want talk a lot because I have so many distractions in my mind. I haven't tried it with other person yet, but I thought it is a useful skill which can make everyone happy.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

The three techniques were: Have a buddy, Do less than you can, and Take one breath a day. I will try all of them. I have a friend who already likes the meditation and she might share the experience with me. Doing less sounds easy, and taking one breath sounds easy as well. I just need to try to not forget everyday.

Any other musings?

I liked walking meditation too. I like waking because it's relaxing and making it easy to organise my thoughts. It will be better with meditation.
