Motivation

How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?

Pleasure is happyness that comes from happy moment but it is hard to maintain, passion comes where you feel your are involved and work performance meets your ability, and highger purpose is like dream. People tends to find pleasure more in their lives but happiness coming from the pleasure is not very sustainable. Hence for sustainable happiness, people should focus on highter purpose, sometimes enjoying flow and then look for pleasure.
