Emotional Intelligence

What learnings did you take from the chapter?

The Emotional Intelligence(EQ) is ability to understand and manage feelings and emotions. There are 5 domains: self-awareness, self-regulation, motivation, empathy, and social skills. It is scientifically proved that optimist has better work performance and leadership and it's also related to happiness. Anyone can improve emotional intelligence by training.

How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

I think the two minutes of mindfulness is good training for the emotional intelligence. I heard that meditation is really good to controlling emotions and enhance concentration from other people as well but sometimes it's hard to make time or remember to do everyday. For that reason, I think two minutes of mindfulness is short and easy way to make happiness and optimize myself. I tried few times before studying, and I found it easy and it helped me to manage my feelings so I could focus better.
