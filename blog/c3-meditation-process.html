Meditation Process

What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2?

I thought meditation helps people to control feelings and emotions by clearing their mind and enhance concentration.

What new things have you learnt about meditation?

Meditation is mental training that can be done by focusing on breathing and gaining attention. It enhances mental health and happiness. It gives detailed instruction about the right posture which needs to be both alert and relaxed and how to meditate properly. That was different what I thought and was interesting.

Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out to you?

I found how to deal with distractions are pretty interesting because When I meditate I always tried hard to get rid of distractions. but it said 'experience without judging' and 'gently inviting it to stop distracting'. Also I liked 'grandmother mind'and becoming friend with myself and I thought it is what I need sometimes.

Any other musings?

It was surprising that meditation even speeds up people's skin recovery.
