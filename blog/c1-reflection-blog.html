Reflection after watching Shereef's Fireside Chat video

What's your take on the EDA experience?

I want to learn lots of web development skills and project skills in team-based environment.

What are your impressions?

In the video, Shereef said about people's attitude in the restaurant and the kitchen to explain creator's mindset. I noticed that I was more like people in the restaurant who are just waiting for someone to help me. I should think more actively and get involved to create my own website and software in order to be a better software developer.

How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

This culture is different from what I had until now and it might be hard for me to understand and follow the new culture. But I think it is a great way to work in team and to express myself to be a good creator.

Have your expectations of EDA changed? If so, how?

Little bit. I expected EDA as just a web development course but I found that they help people in their habit and communication way as well, which is important too.

Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?

Yes. But to be honest, I think I am more nervous than excited. I easily get worried when starting new things and meeting new people. But I am excited when I think about myself being changed after completing this course.
